Editing of cinematic narrative and high-impact reframing for the launch of RDR2's vertical format.

01. Project Challenge

The main objective of this project was to adapt native 21:9 cinematic gameplay to connect effectively with audiences on Instagram and TikTok. The visual and structural challenge lay in maintaining the game's original cinematic atmosphere without cluttering the screen, while optimizing resources for fast-paced mobile consumption.

02. Strategy & Technical Development

Through a process focused on dynamic pacing and precise reframing, specific solutions were implemented using Premiere Pro and After Effects. Meticulous position keyframing was executed for the 4:5 format, ensuring a seamless flow that keeps the viewer's attention locked onto the core action and focal points of the piece.

03. Final Polish & Optimization

To ensure a high-end professional standard, a custom color grading process and cinematic film grain were applied. The final result is a polished asset featuring excellent contrast and fluid playback within the social feed, strategically designed to stand out in its digital environment and retain viewers from the very first second.
