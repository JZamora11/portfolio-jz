OCTANE

Premiere Pro

Creation of aggressive montage and immersive audio engineering for the display of modern automotive energy

01. Project Challenge

The main objective of this project was to transform high-speed automotive stock footage to connect effectively with audiences on social media via vertical reels. The visual and structural challenge lay in maintaining an aggressive editing rhythm and impactful sound design without cluttering the screen, while optimizing resources for fast-paced mobile consumption.

02. Strategy & Technical Development

Through a process focused on precise rhythmic cutting and high-impact sound design, specific solutions were implemented using Premiere Pro. Meticulous position keyframing was executed for the 9:16 format, ensuring a seamless flow that keeps the viewer's attention locked onto the core action and focal points of the piece.

03. Final Polish & Optimization

To ensure a high-end professional standard, Lumetri color grading and visual asset unification were applied. The final result is a polished asset featuring vibrant contrast and seamless audio-visual harmony, strategically designed to stand out in its digital environment and retain viewers from the very first second.
